Eye Tests

Welcome to our eye testing platform. Below, you'll find a variety of tests designed to assess different aspects of your vision, such as sharpness, astigmatism, and blur sensitivity. These tests are easy to perform and can provide valuable insights into your eye health.

1. Snellen Chart Test

The Snellen Chart Test is used to measure your visual acuity. It involves reading letters of various sizes from a standardized chart at a specific distance. This test helps determine if you have normal vision or require corrective lenses.

2. Astigmatism Test

The Astigmatism Test checks for irregularities in the curvature of your cornea, which can cause distorted or blurred vision. The test typically involves looking at a series of lines or patterns.

3. Blur Test

The Blur Test evaluates how well your eyes can focus on an object at varying distances. It helps identify focusing problems and may indicate conditions like myopia or hyperopia.
